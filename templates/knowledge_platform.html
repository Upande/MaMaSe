Knowledge Sharing Platform

The Knowledge Sharing Platform is a key driver for Capacity Development for MaMaSe conservation community targeting policy makers, planners, INRM and IWRM organisations and scientists. It facilitates exchange on practices, and messages that promote ownership, change and results. The features to be included are:


  • (Online) courses offered that are useful for IWRM
  • Online library to search for papers, reports, etc.
  • Presentations and movies
