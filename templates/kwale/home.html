About

The ACC Biodiversity Initiative is aimed at improving Flora and Fauna safety and security in the counties to support structural poverty reduction, sustainable economic growth and conservation of the counties’s ecosystems.

.

In this programme the people and institutions in the counties will be supported in a process of structural change, promoting county-wise economic development that lifts people out of poverty and sets them on a sustainable path to improved well being and self-reliance.
